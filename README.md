# PRIVACY POLICY

**Effective Date:** June 15, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to **Koko EMR**, a Primary Healthcare Electronic Medical Records (EMR) platform designed to support healthcare delivery, patient management, and clinical operations across Primary Healthcare Centers (PHCs) in Nigeria.

Koko EMR ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of personal and health information entrusted to us by patients, healthcare workers, healthcare facilities, and government health agencies.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect information when you use our website, mobile applications, and related healthcare services.

By accessing or using Koko EMR, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### Patient Information

We may collect the following patient information:

* Full name
* Date of birth
* Gender
* Phone number
* Residential address
* Next-of-kin information
* National Identification Number (where applicable)
* Health insurance information
* Unique patient identifiers

### Medical and Health Information

To provide healthcare services, we may collect:

* Medical history
* Clinical notes
* Diagnoses
* Vital signs
* Treatment plans
* Consultation records
* Laboratory requests and results
* Prescription records
* Pharmacy dispensing records
* Referral records
* Immunization records
* Maternal and child health records
* Admission and discharge information

### Healthcare Worker Information

We may collect:

* Name
* Professional role
* Employment information
* Facility assignment
* Professional registration numbers
* Login credentials

### Technical Information

When you use Koko EMR, we may automatically collect:

* Device information
* Browser information
* IP address
* Operating system
* Login timestamps
* Usage logs
* System activity records
* Error reports

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

* Register and manage patients
* Provide healthcare services
* Maintain electronic medical records
* Support consultations and clinical workflows
* Manage laboratory and pharmacy operations
* Schedule appointments
* Facilitate referrals
* Generate healthcare reports and statistics
* Monitor healthcare service delivery
* Improve platform functionality
* Provide technical support
* Comply with healthcare regulations and legal obligations

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal and health information where:

* Consent has been provided;
* Processing is necessary for healthcare delivery;
* Processing is required by law;
* Processing is necessary to protect the vital interests of patients;
* Processing is necessary for public health purposes.

---

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell patient information.

Information may be shared only with:

### Healthcare Providers

Authorized doctors, nurses, pharmacists, laboratory personnel, and other healthcare workers involved in patient care.

### Healthcare Facilities

Authorized Primary Healthcare Centres, hospitals, and referral facilities using Koko EMR.

### Government and Regulatory Authorities

Where required by applicable healthcare laws, regulations, public health reporting requirements, or lawful government requests.

### Technology Service Providers

Trusted service providers who support hosting, infrastructure, security, analytics, and system maintenance.

All third-party providers are required to maintain appropriate confidentiality and security measures.

---

## 6. Data Security

Koko EMR implements industry-standard safeguards to protect personal and health information, including:

* Encrypted data transmission
* Role-based access controls
* Secure authentication systems
* Audit logs and activity monitoring
* Data backup and disaster recovery procedures
* Secure cloud infrastructure
* Regular security assessments

Access to patient records is restricted to authorized personnel with legitimate healthcare or administrative purposes.

---

## 7. Data Retention

Patient records and healthcare information are retained in accordance with:

* Applicable Nigerian healthcare regulations;
* Medical record retention requirements;
* Public health reporting obligations;
* Contractual obligations with healthcare facilities.

Information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill these purposes.
